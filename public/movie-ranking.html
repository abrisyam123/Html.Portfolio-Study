My Top 3 Movies of all time

World War Z

I love it because the zombie logic kind of make sense. They are not the usual slow walking movies which in my opinion would just be decimated by the modern army. Instead these are fast moving or as we would call it runners. They ignore their well being to chase you down and acts more like a virus looking for its host. This movie has alot of its clitche and i let it slide.

28 Days later

This was one of my beloved zombie movie as i saw this growing up, cult classic. This movies actually instill the feeling of fear and dread which is hard to get when watching a zombie movie, well atleast for me. The soundtract for this movie is so peak its insane. The soundtract alone made some of the scene so intense. Worth a watch.

Shaun of the dead

I love this movie so much as a kid and i still do now, an awesome combination of comedy and ultraviolence. This is another cult classic in my opinion, its so good.

I am legend

This one is special because they are not necessarily zombies, watch the movie to figure it out. A very good twist to the movie, a lot of drama. It really makes you think deep once you understand what was really going on
